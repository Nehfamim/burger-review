Vetrina Review

Well, what can we say already?

Vitrina is a thing.
Everything there is excellent and hasn't changed for years. No matter what you order - it's delicious at the top 10 level in Israel and many will say it's also at the top of the table.
A real consensus.

Don't miss their chicken either because it's worth another visit.
Best? Come with a friend and take one hamburger and one chicken and share both. Mush fries and dips are amazing too. Everything everything is excellent
